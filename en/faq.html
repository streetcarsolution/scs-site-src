Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know for a 100% worry-free rental.

Booking

How to book?
  • Choose your vehicle on our website, then complete the pre-booking form.
  • We will contact you promptly via WhatsApp to finalize the booking details and assign you a vehicle.
What documents do I need to provide to book?
  • Your driver's license and passport are required for all reservations, as well as round-trip flight tickets.
  • For Mexican residents, a driver's license and INE card are required..
Do I need an international driver's license?
No matter your nationality, an international driver's license is not mandatory. For more information, please visit the official website of your general consulate in Mexico.
What is the minimum age to book?
  • The minimum age is 21, or 25 for certain vehicle models. The driver's license must have been valid for at least 2 years.
  • * Rental is possible for young drivers with an additional fee. Contact us for more information.
What is the minimum number of days I can book?
We rent for a minimum of five days. The exception is for pick-up and drop-off in Cancun, where we can rent for four or three days, subject to availability.
In what language is the rental agreement written?
All rental agreements are written in Spanish, according to Mexican law. Don't worry, one of our advisors will translate it for you if needed.
Can I modify or cancel my reservation?
Yes, contact us as soon as possible. Modifications depend on availability. Cancellation conditions are specified in our Terms & Conditions.

Insurance & Payments

What insurance is included?
  • Third-party liability (RC) and medical assistance are included.
  • Collision Damage Waiver (CDW) and Theft Protection (TP) with a 10% deductible on the value of the vehicle are also included.
  • We recommend you subscribe to our "24/7 Police Assistance Service" for complete peace of mind during checks with local authorities.
What is the security deposit amount?
The security deposit is between $3,000 Pesos and $7,000 Pesos, depending on the vehicle category. The deposit is blocked upon vehicle delivery and will be released upon return after the vehicle has been checked.
Do I need to pay for my reservation in advance?
The full payment for the rental is made in person when you sign the contract and we hand over the vehicle keys.
What payment methods do you accept?
  • We accept payments in cash or by bank card (Debit/Credit). Bank card numbers are not stored on our website. A 5% bank fee is added for all card payments.
  • The rates are set in Mexican Pesos, but we can accept foreign currencies based on the daily exchange rate on the day of payment.

Vehicle Use

Is mileage unlimited?
Yes—within the states of Quintana Roo, Yucatán and Campeche, so you can explore all the amazing sites across the region.
Is there a spare wheel in the car?
All our vehicles include a spare wheel and the tools needed to change a tire.
Can I drive with a photocopy of my license?
No. A photocopy is not an official document and the police may require the original. Always carry your physical driver’s license to avoid fines or complications.
Are your license plates local?
Yes, all our vehicles have local plates.
Additional drivers
A second driver is free of charge. Additional drivers can be added for a small fee per extra driver.
Can I bring my pet?
Yes—pets are welcome at Street Car Solution. Please protect the interior and return the vehicle clean.
What should I do in case of breakdown, accident or a fine?
  • Contact us immediately—24/7. We’ll assist you with roadside help, reports and guidance.
  • Consider the optional “24/7 Police Assistance” service for direct support during complex checks with law enforcement.

Ferry, Borders & Restricted Areas

Parking & ferry
Ferry terminals in Chiquilá, Cancún and Playa del Carmen all have secure paid parking lots.
Cozumel: can I take the ferry with the vehicle?
Yes—vehicles can cross to Cozumel by ferry. Prices vary depending on the operator.
Holbox: can I drive onto the island?
  • No vehicle ferries operate to Holbox. Please park in Chiquilá at one of the lots near the passenger ferry. Typical rate: ~$100 MXN/day.
  • Important: keep your car key with you.
Chiapas: can I drive to Palenque?
Yes, but you must inform us in advance; a supplement applies. Contact us for details.
Can I cross the Mexican border to Belize?
No—taking the vehicle outside Mexico is prohibited and not covered by insurance.
Punta Allen / Sian Ka'an: can I drive there?
No—off-road tracks in these areas are excluded from insurance coverage; damage or accidents are not covered.

Safety, Roads & Climate

Is Mexico safe for travelers?
  • Yes—especially the Yucatán Peninsula, which hosts millions of visitors every year and a large expat community.
  • Locals are welcoming and used to tourism; as everywhere, common-sense precautions apply.
Can I drive at night?
Yes—many main roads are lit. Always adapt your speed and stay alert.
Is vehicle theft common?
To date, we have not experienced a single vehicle theft.
Are child seats mandatory?
Mexican law requires child restraint systems for minors under 12. In practice there is tolerance for locals, but we strongly recommend a child seat for any kid under 4 for safety.
What are the road conditions like?
Major highways are in good condition; some roads or streets can be rough. Watch for potholes and topes (speed bumps)!
What’s the climate like in Quintana Roo / Yucatán?
  • Mostly hot and humid. The rainy season typically spans August to October. November–February is milder, then heat returns through August.
  • Tropical showers are intense but brief; the sun usually returns quickly and roads dry fast. Several hours of continuous rain are uncommon but can happen.

Delivery & Return

How can I be sure a car will be ready on arrival?
Once you receive our rental confirmation message from an advisor, your vehicle is guaranteed and will be delivered to you upon arrival.
Where can I pick up and return the car?
Airport, hotel or an agreed meeting point in Cancún and across the Riviera Maya. Please share your flight/meeting details at booking for a smooth handover.
Late return: how is it handled?
Let us know as early as possible. A grace period of up to 1 hour may apply depending on availability; beyond that, daily extra charges apply.
Fuel policy
Same fuel level at pick-up and return. If returned with less fuel, refuelling and a service fee will be charged.
