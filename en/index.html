Street Car Solution

Tired of complicated car rentals?

Founded in 2018 by Yoan Cremona, a French entrepreneur in Cancún, Street Car Solution was created to make travel easier for French-speaking visitors.

Our mission: deliver a simple, transparent rental experience tailored to your needs — without the usual hurdles like the “credit card only” policy often required by international brands.

Thanks to our deep knowledge of both French and Mexican cultures, we advise you on the best way to explore the Riviera Maya with confidence.

We carefully select each vehicle in our fleet to ensure comfort, safety and reliability during your trip.
